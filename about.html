ABOUT

Hello! My name is Giovanni Leone.

I am a Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania native and huge fan of the Pittsburgh Penguins and Steelers.

Currently, I attend Rochester Institute of Technology as a third-year Graphic Design major. My art courses contribute to my diverse knowledge of print design, web design, and motion graphics. In addition, to my art courses, I have a concentration in Communications and have taken courses involving mass, interpersonal, and human communication.

RIT's Sustainability Department employs my services as a designer for numerous print and web projects that directly affect the community and campus. I created various labels and posters that are featured around campus and during events.
